1st Cardiff Image and Vision Computing Workshop
Workshop organised by the Cardiff Image and Vision Computing Group, June 2nd, 2025 (Cardiff, Wales, UK)

The Cardiff Image and Vision Computing Group is organising an in-person workshop on image and vision computing. It will take place on 1 June 2025 in Abacws, the brand-new building for the School of Computer Science and the School of Mathematics at Cardiff University.

The workshop is especially intended to image and vision computing practitioners and researchers, but everyone would be welcome. Unfortunately, the registration period is now closed. A draft schedule is now available.

Follow our updates on Twitter! @CardiffVisComp #cardiffcvworkshop

TARGET AUDIENCE

The workshop is targeted to an audience with some prior knowledge of Image and Vision Computing.

PhD students and early career researchers working on Image and Vision Computing are especially encouraged to attend.

WORKSHOP ACTIVITY

Invited speakers | Tutorials | Networking | Panels

IMPORTANT DATES

Workshop (in-person only): Cardiff, 2 June 2024

This event is organised by the Cardiff Image and Vision Computing Group and supported by Cardiff University's School of Computer Science and Informatics.
